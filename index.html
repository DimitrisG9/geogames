Thief's Shelter

Thief's Shelter is an immersive adventure that plunges players into a richly atmospheric world where every choice matters. The game combines a gripping narrative, complex puzzles and various other elements in an ultra-modernized point-and-click adventure fused with first person elements in an ever-changing 3D environment.

The Developer Story

Dimitris Georgantis - Geo Games

I'm all about crafting memorable experiences that allow people to escape and feel immersed. I'm mostly honing my motion design skills because I am a sucker for animations and timelines/sequences. Thief's Shelter serves as an introduction to its universe and rules, inviting players to navigate a challenging situation through the lens of a flawed character like James and overcome his artificial dementia to acquire as much knowledge as possible. Its full story will unfold cross the trilogy. Additionally, I would also love to work on a Souls-like project that includes storytelling elements, making the audience attached to the characters and its combat nuances.
